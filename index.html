Universe introduction


Join us on an adventure inside the busy life of NFT giraffes

  • Firefighter to the rescue

    Since it is one of the most difficult pieces in our collection we would like to start our presentation with the Firefighter Giraffe. We take pride in generating NFT's that represent our daily lives and the professions we take for granted. Without these roles modern society can't be sustained. Only love for the flame guardians.

  • Mission impossible

    Collection wise we wouldn't be able to launch without the Police Officer Giraffe because it represents the idea of public safety, which is a troubling subject at the moment. Thank you for protecting our Giraffe family, Officer!

  • A productive day on site

    The artwork for this piece has elements of a young, active and upcoming personality. This is a close representation of our young adults that have started their life and have already managed to reach a point of higher evolution in our society.

  • A busy day at the doctor's office

    We can't look over the fact that without this profession we wouldn't be able to enjoy our big Giraffe families together for as long as possible. The artwork took about 2 full days to be realized as our graphic designer first sketched the frames then made the colored Giraffe to represent how they manage to stay colored even though the background that nobody sees is sometimes cold therefore blue.

  • Butcher on duty

    Food is a subject of the heart as the ones that work so we can taste that great steak with our friends and families are pivotal members of the longneck society. Get your order from the local Butcher and you will have a feast.

  • A difficult case

    The protectors of democracy assure the justice we all need and want. The pillars of society are represented by the doctors, lawyers and engineers that we all wanted to have implemented in our collection.

  • Deep-sea diving

    Fun is never a bad idea. Let us go on a trip through the deeps with this ace artwork and energetic vibe that give you the taste of exotic adventures. Make it look live, happy and fun! Say no moreeee!

  • Journey to the Moon

    As the biggest “Would but can't” dreams of our development team we decided to create the imagine of what space would feel like for our Giraffe to discover. The points we can't reach are the ideas that stick with us our whole life. Think big, fellow Giraffe!

  • An important lesson from the mountains

    Most of us live in mountain regions of Europe and Asia as we would like to add some homely energy to the collection and bring you closer to the chilling feeling of winter. Mix in your imagination through the artwork and set yourself free from the walls you live in.

  • The corporatist with a suitcase

    Business is our main activity but now it comes last as we have managed to finish our collection with the best of what we can do and produce. Little pieces every day so we can think more highly of the moments that follow.
